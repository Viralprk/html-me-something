Buddha1

I was always inspired by Budhha and wanted to make a painting that is relaxing and soothing - finally settled for this with Buddha statue. It resonates calm and peace to me. I choose oil paints and blending techniques. One of his inspiration queotes- Educate yourself in order to be a high ranking official of any entity. Every master starts as a servant. Keep learning to be promoted. But keep your feet always on the ground no matter what. Because the people you meet on your way up will be same people you will meet on your way down. Be humble.
